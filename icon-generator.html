Extension Icon Generator

This tool will generate PNG icons from the SVG icon for your browser extension.

16x16

48x48

128x128

Instructions:

  1. Click "Generate Icons" to create the PNG icons from the SVG
  2. Click "Download All" to download all three icon sizes
  3. Place the downloaded files in the extension/icons/ folder
  4. Rename them to: icon16.png, icon48.png, and icon128.png
